The problem

The old career ladder depended on the grind.

Students were told routine work would teach them how to become valuable. AI is now compressing or automating much of that work. The classroom needs a new way to teach professional identity, judgment, and human contribution.

The module

A complete class session in one kit.

Students take the assessment before class, arrive with their top archetypes, then use the session to connect the 4I Framework to real career choices and an actionable 30-day development plan.

Best fit Upper-division business courses, career centers, first-year experience, executive education, AI and society modules.
Time 45-minute lecture, 75-minute class plan, or 90-minute workshop.
Student output A post-grind value plan tied to each student's top archetypes.

Learning outcomes

Students leave with language and a plan.

  • Explain why AI changes entry-level work and career development.
  • Describe the 4I Framework of human value.
  • Identify their top Human Work Spectrum archetypes.
  • Define one skill, one habit, and one AI boundary to practice for 30 days.
