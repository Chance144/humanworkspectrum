# Archetype Group Activity

## Purpose

Help students move from "this is my result" to "this is how I create value."

## Group Setup

Option A:
Group students by primary archetype.

Option B:
If groups are too small, group by domain:

- Interpretive
- Integrative
- Interpersonal
- Imaginative

## Instructions

Each group answers the following questions.

### 1. What do we notice?

What does this archetype tend to notice before other people do?

Examples:

- Bad assumptions.
- Weak trust.
- Poor sequencing.
- Missing meaning.
- Noisy dashboards.
- Second-order consequences.

### 2. What AI failure do we catch?

What kind of mistake or misuse of AI is this archetype especially likely to catch?

Examples:

- Confident but shallow output.
- Automation without accountability.
- Data without meaning.
- Adoption without trust.
- Efficiency that creates long-term harm.

### 3. What do we help teams do?

Complete this sentence:

In a workplace where AI does routine work, this archetype helps teams...

### 4. What do we need to practice?

Name one skill someone would need to build to become credible in this archetype.

### 5. Report-out

Each group shares:

- One sentence about what the archetype notices.
- One sentence about what the archetype helps teams do.
- One skill the archetype needs to practice.
