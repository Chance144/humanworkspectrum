# 75-Minute Class Plan

Title: Human Work After the Grind

## Before Class

Students complete:

1. Human Work Spectrum assessment.
2. My Spectrum dashboard review.
3. Student pre-work reflection.

## Materials

- Projector.
- Whiteboard.
- Archetype group activity.
- Post-Grind Value Plan assignment.

## Class Flow

### 0-8 minutes: Opening

Prompt:

What is one task you expected to do early in your career that AI can already do reasonably well?

Collect examples.

Transition:

Those tasks used to be more than tasks. They were part of how people learned judgment.

### 8-18 minutes: Mini-Lecture

Cover:

- The old bargain: do the grind, earn judgment.
- The new problem: AI compresses the grind.
- The new question: what human value gets built instead?

Key line:

AI is not just changing tasks. It is changing the path by which people become valuable.

### 18-30 minutes: 4I Framework

Explain the four domains:

- Interpretive: meaning and judgment.
- Integrative: systems and synthesis.
- Interpersonal: trust and human development.
- Imaginative: futures and possibility.

Ask students to rank the four domains by how much they expect to use them in their first five years after graduation.

### 30-42 minutes: Archetype Groups

Students move into groups by primary archetype or closest domain.

Each group answers:

1. What does this archetype notice that others miss?
2. What kind of AI misuse is this archetype likely to catch?
3. What does this archetype help a team do better?
4. What skill would someone need to practice to become credible in this archetype?

### 42-55 minutes: Group Report-Out

Each group gets two minutes.

Ask them to complete this sentence:

In a workplace where AI does routine work, our archetype creates value by...

### 55-68 minutes: Individual Value Plan

Students start the Post-Grind Value Plan in class.

Required writing:

1. My primary archetype:
2. My supporting archetype:
3. The kind of work I should seek:
4. The skill I need to build:
5. The habit I need to practice:
6. The AI boundary I need to set:

### 68-75 minutes: Close

Ask:

What is one thing you should stop saying about your career after today?

Possible answers:

- I am just good at marketing.
- I am just a business student.
- I will figure it out when I get a job.
- AI is just a tool.

Close:

Your job is not to compete with AI at the grind. Your job is to become legible as a human source of value.
